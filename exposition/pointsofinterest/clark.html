Statue of William Clark

Location in 1905: 45.53868662, -122.70704582
In the sculpture, Clark is carrying a sword he gave to a Walla Walla chief in exchange for a white horse and other assistance.
Frederick Wellington Ruckstuhl was a French-born sculptor and a founder of the National Sculpture Society. He was awarded a grand medal for his sculpture "Evening" at the 1893 World's Columbian Exposition.
