AJC And he was in the wilderness for forty days and forty nights: and was tested by Satan: and was with the wild beasts, and angels attended to him.

AJC But after John was handed over, Jesus went to Galilee, preaching the Gospel of the kingdom of God.

AJC and saying: Since the time has been fulfilled, and the kingdom of God has drawn nigh, repent ye, and believe in the Gospel.

AJC And passing by the Sea of Galilee, he saw Simon, and his brother Andrew, casting their nets into the sea (for they were fishermen),

AJC and Jesus said to them: Come after me, and I will cause you to become fishers of men.

AJC And immediately abandoning their nets, they followed him.

AJC And after he had gone on from there just a bit, he saw Jacob Zebedaiah, and his brother John, and they were stowing their nets in their boat:

AJC and he straightway called them. And leaving their father Zebedaiah in the boat with the hired men, they followed him.

AJC And they entered Capharnaum: and on the Sabbath Jesus straightway entered the synagogue, and taught them.

AJC And they were astonished at his teaching: for he was teaching them as if he had power, and not like the scribes.

AJC And there was in their synagogue a man with an unclean spirit: and he shouted,

AJC saying: What is it for us and thee, Jesus of Nazareth? Habe you come to destroy us? I know who you are, Holy Man of God.
