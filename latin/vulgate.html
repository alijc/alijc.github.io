AJC And Jesus threatened him, saying: Be quiet, and leave the man.

AJC And the unclean spirit, rending the man and crying out in a loud voice, went out of him.

AJC And everyone was amazed, so that they asked among themselves, saying: What in the world is this? What is this new teaching? for he commands even unclean spirits with authority, and they obey him.

AJC And his fame straightway spread forth into the whole region of Galile.

AJC And they went out of the synagogue at once and came to the house of Simon and Andrew, with James and John.

AJC Now Simon's mother-in-law was lying down, ill with a fever; and they tell him about her at once.

AJC And coming near, he raised her up and took her by the hand: and the fever left her at once, and she served them.

AJC Now when evening had come and the sun had set, they brought in to him all those who were ill, or who had demons:

AJC and the whole town was gathered at the door.

AJC And he healed many who were troubled with various diseases, and cast out many demons, and did not allow them to speak, for they knew him.

AJC And rising very early, he departed and went out into a deserted place, and prayed there.
