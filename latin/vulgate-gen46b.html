AJC These are the sons of Zelpha, whom Laban gave to his daughter Leah: and she bore to Jacob these sixteen souls.

AJC The sons of Jacob's wife Rachel: Joseph and Benjamin.

AJC And sons were born to Joseph in the land of Egypt, that Aseneth the daughter of Putiphare the priest of Heliopoleos bore for him: Manasses and Ephraim.

AJC The sons of Benjamin: Bela and Bechor and Asbel and Gera and Naaman and Echi and Ros and Mophim and Ophim and Ared.

AJC These are the sons that Rachel bore for Jacob: all the souls were fourteen.

AJC The sons of Dan: Husim.

AJC The sons of Nephthali: Jasiel and Guni and Jasiel and Sallem.

AJC These are the sons of Bala, whom Laban gave to his daughter Rachel: And she bore these for Jacob: all the souls were seven.

AJC All the souls that entered with Jacob into Egypt, and came out of his loins, apart from the wives of his sons, were sixty-six.

AJC And the sons of Joseph, who were born to him in the land of Egypt, were two souls. All the souls of the house of Jacob, who came into Egypt, were seventy.

AJC And he sent Juda before him to Joseph, to tell him that he would meet him in Gessen.

AJC When he had arrived, Joseph harnessed his chariot and went up to his father on the way to that same place; and seeing him, fell upon his neck, and wept while they embraced.

AJC And his father said to Joseph: I will die happy, for I have seen thy face, and you will still live when I am gone.

AJC But he said to his brothers, and to the entire house of his father: I will go up and announce to Pharaoh, and will tell him: My brothers, and my father's house, who were in the land of Canaan, have come to me:

AJC and the men are shepherds, and have the responsibility for maintaining flocks: their cattle, and herds, and all that they possessed, they have brought with them.

AJC And when he calls ye, and says: What is your work?

AJC Ye shall answer: We thy servants are herdsmen, from our infancy up to the present time, both us and our fathers. And say these things, so that ye might live in the land of Gessen: for the Egyptians destest all sheepherders.
