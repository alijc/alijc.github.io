1 AJC Joseph could not restrain himself before the many men standing there: he ordered them all to go out of there, and have no stranger present at the mutual acknowledgement.

2 AJC And he lifted his voice with weeping, which the Egyptians heard, and the whole house of Pharaoh.

3 AJC And he said to his brothers: I am Joseph: does my father yet live? His brothers were so terrified that they could not reply.

4 AJC He said to them gently: Come nearer to me. And when they had approached near: I am, he said, Joseph, your brother, whom you sold into Egypt.

5 AJC Fear not, nor should it seem to you to be a cruel thing that you sold me into these lands: for God sent me before you into Egypt for the sake of your prosperity.

6 AJC For it's two years since famine began on the earth: and yet five years remain, during which there will be neither tilling nor reaping.

7 AJC And God sent me ahead in order that you would be spared upon the earth, and might be able to have victuals to live on.
