1 AJC Then Joseph gave an order to the steward of his house, saying: Fill up their sacks with grain, as much as they can hold. And put each one's money in the top of the sack.

2 AJC And my silver goblet, and the price that he gave for wheat, put into the mouth of the sack of the youngest. And so it was done.

3 AJC And at sunrise, they were sent away, along with their asses.

4 AJC And now, after they had gone out of the city, and had proceeded a little: then Joseph, having sent for the steward of the house, said "Rise up, and overtake the men:" and when they've been seized, say: Why have you repaid evil for good?

5 AJC the goblet, which you have stolen, is the very one in which my lord drinks, and in which he is wont to practice augury: you have done a most wicked thing.

6 AJC He did as he had been ordered: and when they were seized spoke as ordered.

7 AJC And they replied: Why does our lord say so, that thy servants would commit such an outrage?

8 AJC the money that we found in the top of our sacks, we carried back to thee from the land of Canaan: how would it be that afterwards we would steal gold or silver from the house of thy lord?

9 AJC with whichever of thy servants might be found what thou seeks, let him die, and we will become slaves of our lord.
