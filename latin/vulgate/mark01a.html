AJC The beginning of the Gospel of Jesus Christ, the Son of God.

AJC As it was written in the prophet Isaiah: Behold I send my angel before thy face, who will prepare thy way before thee.

AJC A voice crying in the wilderness: Prepare ye the way of the Lord, make ye his paths straight.

AJC John was baptising in the wilderness, and preaching the baptism of repentance in forgiveness of sins.

AJC And everyone in the land of Judea came out to him, and everyone from Jerusalem, and were baptized by him in the river of Jordan, confessing their sins.

AJC And John was dressed in camel hair, and a belt of leather around his loins, and he ate locusts and wild honey.

AJC And he preached, saying: There comes after me one who is mightier, whose shoelaces I am not worthy to lean down and untie.

AJC I have baptized you with water, that one will truly baptize you with the Holy Spirit.

AJC And it was done: in those days Jesus came from Nazareth of Galilee: and was baptized by John in the Jordan.

AJC And just as he rose up out of the water, he saw the opened heavens, and the Spirit descending like a dove, and remaining on him.

AJC And a voice was produced from the heavens: Thou are my beloved Son, I did please in thee. (This would make more sense to me if it was passive.)
