AJC May Dan be a serpent on the road, a snake in the path, biting the horse's hoof, so that its rider falls on his back.

AJC I will await thy salvation, Lord.

AJC Gad, belted, did battle before him: and was belted backwards.

AJC Aser is a rich loaf of bread, and will supply delights to kings.

AJC Nephthali is a driven stag, and uttering speeches of beauty.

AJC Joseph, is a rising son, rising and glorious to behold: the daughters rushed about on the wall.

AJC But the men who held the darts irritated and envied him and were scolded.

AJC His bow rested on the mighty, and the chains of his hands and arms were loosed through the hands of mighty Jacob: a shepherd went out of that place, the stone of Israel.

AJC The God of thy father will be thy help, and the Almighty will bless thee with blessings of the heaven above, with blessings of the deep-lying sea, with blessings of the breasts and womb.

AJC The blessings of thy father are strengthened by the blessings of his fathers, until might come the desire of the everlasting hills: may these things be done on Joseph's head, and on the summit of Nazareth among his brothers.

AJC Benjamin is a ravenous wolf, may he eat his prey in the morning, and divide the spoils in the evening.

AJC All these things in the 12 tribes of Isreal: their father said to them, and blessed each one with his proper blessing.

AJC And he taught them, saying: I am being collected to my people: bury me with my fathers in the double cave which is in the field of Ephron the Hittite,

AJC opposite Mambrus in the land of Canaan, that Abraham bought along with the field from Ephron the Hittite for a tomb.

AJC There they buried Abraham, and his wife Sara: there Isaac was buried with his wife Rebecca: and there Leah lies buried.

AJC And having finished the commands with which he instructed his sons, he drew his feet up onto the bed, and died: and he was gathered to his people.
