AJC And after Joseph had lifted them from his father's lap, he worshipped, facedown on the ground.

AJC And he put Ephraim on his right hand, that is, on Israel's left: and Manasses on his left hand, to his father's right, and brought both near to Jacob.

AJC And he, putting forth his right hand, put it on the head of Ephraim, the younger brother: and the left on the head of Manasses, who was the elder by birth, rearranging his hands.

AJC And Jacob blessed the sons of Joseph, and said: God, in whose sight Abraham and Isaac, my forefathers, sojourned; God, who has fed me from my youth up to the present day:

AJC let the angel, who plucked me up from everything evil, bless these boys: and let my name be invoked upon them, likewise the names of my forefathers Abraham and Isaac, and let them grow into a multitude upon the earth.

AJC But when Joseph saw that his father had put his right hand on Ephraim's head, he took it ill: and seized his father's hand and tried to lift it from Ephraim's head and move it unto Manasses'.

AJC And he said to his father: It is fit thus, father: because this one is the first-born, put thy right hand on his head.

AJC And he refused, saying: I know, my son, I know: and indeed this one will become into crowds, and will be multiplied: but his younger brother, will be greater yet: and his seed will increase into nations.

AJC And he blessed them then, saying: In thee Israel will be blessed, and it will be said: Let God create for thee one like Ephraim, and like Manasses. And he placed Ephraim before Manasses.

AJC And he said to his son Joseph: Behold, I die, and God will be with you. and He will restore you to the land of your fathers.

AJC I give to thee one share outside of thy brothers, as I brought from the hand of Amorites with my sword and bow.
