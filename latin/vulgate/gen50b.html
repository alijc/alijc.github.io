AJC And Joseph returned to Egypt with his brothers and his whole company after burying his father.

AJC Now that he was dead his brothers were afraid and said among themselves: Lest Joseph perhaps be mindful of the wrong that he endured, and pay us back for all the evil that we brought about.

AJC they commanded him, saying: Before he died thy father instructed us,

AJC to say this to thee, with these words: I beseech thee to forget thy brothers' crime, and the sin and wickedness that they imposed on thee: likewise we pray that thou let go of this iniquitity by the servants of the God of thy father. And when he heard this, Joseph wept.

AJC And his brothers came to him: and falling flat on the ground, said: We are thy servants.

AJC And he answered them: Fear not: can we really withstand the will of God?

AJC You intended evil with regard to me: but God turned that into good, to raise me up, as you now see, and to make many people safe.

AJC Fear not: I will feed you and your little ones: and he consoled them, and spoke gently and mildly.

AJC And he dwelt in Egypt with all of his father's house: and lived 110 years. And saw the sons of Ephraim up to the third generation. Likewise the sons of Machir the son of Manassus were born on Joseph's knees.

AJC After these things had happened, he said to his brothers: After my death God will visit you, and cause you to go up out of this land to the land that he swore to Abraham, Isaac and Jacob.

AJC And when he had sworn to them and had said: God will visit you, carry my bones away from this place with you:

AJC he died, having completed 110 years of his life. And after being embalmed, was laid to rest in Egypt.
