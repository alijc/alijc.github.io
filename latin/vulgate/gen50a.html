AJC When Joseph saw this, he fell upon his father's face, weeping and kissing him.

AJC And he commanded his servants the doctors to embalm his father with spices.

AJC And while they fulfilled his decrees, 40 days passed; as was naturally the custom for embalmed bodies: and Egypt mourned him for 70 days.

AJC And when the time of mourning was completed, Joseph said to Pharaoh's family: If I have found favor in your sight, say so in Pharaoh's hearing:

AJC For my father charged me, saying: "Lo, I die; you will bury me in my tomb, which I dug for myself in the land of Canaan." Therefore I will go up, and will bury my father, and then return.

AJC And Pharaoh said to him: Go up, and bury your father, as you were charged.

AJC And as he went up, there went with him all the old men of the house of Pharaoh. and every elder by birth of the land of Egypt:

AJC the house of Joseph with his brothers, except for the children, and the flocks and herd, which they left behind in the land of Goshen.

AJC Likewise he had in his company chariots and horsemen: and it became a not small crowd.

AJC And they came to the Atad threshing floor, which lay across the Jordan: where performing the funeral rites with great and vehement lamentation took up seven days.

AJC After the inhabitants of the land of Canaan had seen this, they said: That man was greatly mourned by the Egyptians. And on that account the name of that place was called, Egypt's Lamentation.

AJC And so the sons of Jacob did as he had commanded them:

AJC and after carrying him into the land of Canaan, they buried him in the double tomb, that Abraham had bought with the field from Ephron the Hittite for the possession of a tomb, opposite the face of Mambre.
