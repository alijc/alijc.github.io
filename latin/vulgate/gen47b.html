AJC And he answered them: Bring me your livestock, and I will give you food for it, if you have no money.

AJC When they had brought it, he gave them provisions in return for their horses, and sheep, and oxen, and asses: and he sustained them for that year in exchange for their livestock.

AJC Likewise, they came in the second year, and said to him: We'll not hide from our lord that our money is gone, and our livestock is gone too: nor is it unknown to thee, that except for our bodies and our land, we have nothing.

AJC Why shall we then die in thy sight? Both us and our land will be thine: Buy us into the king's servitude, and supply us with grain, lest, lacking tillers, the land be reduced to a wilderness.

AJC Then Joseph bought all the land of Egypt, with each man selling his possessions on account of the magnitude of the famine. And he placed it under Pharaoh.

AJC also all of its people, from one end of Egypt to the other,

AJC except for the land of the priests, which was bequeathed to them by the king: and it had been established that they were to be supplied rations from the common store, and on that account they were not compelled to sell their possessions.

AJC Then Joseph said to the people: Lo! just as you see, Pharaoh possesses both you and your land: take seed, and sow the fields,

AJC that you might have crops. You will give a fifth part to the king; the remaining four parts are entrusted to you for planting, and for food for your families and children.

AJC And they replied: Our health is in thy hand: Let our lord pay us such great respect, and we will be glad and serve the king.

AJC From that time up to the present day, in all the land of Egypt a fifth part is released to the king, just as if this was brought about in law, except for the land of the priests, which was free of this condition.

AJC So Israel lived in Egypt, that is, in the land of Gessen, and was master of the land: and was increased and multiplied a great deal.

AJC And he lived on that land seventeen years: and the days of his life were accomplished, 147 years.

AJC And when he sensed that the day of his death was near, he called his son Joseph, and said to him: If I have found favor in thy sight, put thy hand under my thigh: and thou wilt do for me mercy and truth, that I not be buried in Egypt:

AJC but let me sleep with my fathers, and may thou carry me from this land, and bury me in the sepulcher of my ancestors. And Joseph answered him: I will do what you have commanded.

AJC And he said: Then swear it to me. And while he swore, Israel turned to the head of the bed and worshiped God.
