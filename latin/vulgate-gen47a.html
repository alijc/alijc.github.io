AJC Then Joseph went in and reported to Pharaoh, saying: My father and brothers, their sheep and their cattle, and all that they possess, have come from the land of Canaan: and lo they remain in the land of Gessen.

AJC He also brought his last five brothers into the presence of the king:

AJC He asked them: What is your occupation? They replied: We thy servants are shepherds, both we and our fathers.

AJC We came into thy country to sojourn in a strange land. because there is no grass for thy servants' flocks. with the famine growing heavier in the land of Canaan: and we pray that thou order us thy servants to live in the land of Gessen.

AJC And so the king said to Joseph: Thy father and thy brothers have come to thee.

AJC The land of Egypt is within thy view: cause them to dwell in the best place, and deliver to them the land of Gessen. But if thou have found among them industrious men, set them up as masters of my flocks.

AJC After these things Joseph brought in his father to the king, and set him in his presence: and he blessed him,

AJC and asked of him: How many are the days of the years of thy life?

AJC he replied: The days of my wanderings are one hundred and thirty, few, and ill, and they don't come up to the days of my fathers which were spent in wandering.

AJC And after he blessed the king, he went outside.

AJC Indeed Joseph gave his father and brothers property in Egypt in the best place in the land, Ramesses, just as Pharaoh had instructed.

AJC And he nourished them, and all of his father's house, supplying food to each one.

AJC For bread was wanting in the whole world, and famine had overwhelmed the land, especially Egypt and Canaan.

AJC He collected from them all the money for selling grain, and brought it into the king's treasury.

AJC And when the price to the buyers fell short, all Egypt came to Joseph, saying: Give us bread: why should we die in thy presence, lacking money.
