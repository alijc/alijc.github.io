AJC After Israël had set out with all that he had, he came to the Well of the Covenant; and after making sacrifices there to the God of his father Isaac,

AJC he heard Him in a night vision, summoning him and saying to him: Jacob, Jacob. And he responded: Behold, I am here.

AJC God said to him: I am the most powerful God of thy father: fear not, go down into Egypt, for I will make thee into a great nation there.

AJC I will go down to that place with thee, and I will bring thee back on thy return: and Joseph will likewise put his hands on thy eyes.

AJC Then Jacob rose up from the Well of the Covenant: and bore his sons with their children and wives in the carts that Pharaoh had sent for carrying the old man,

AJC and all that he had owned in the land of Canaan: and he came into Egypt with his whole seed,

AJC his sons, and grandsons, daughters, and all his progeny together.

AJC Now these are the names of the children of Israël, who went into Egypt, themselves with their children. The firstborn Ruben.

AJC The sons of Ruben: Henoch and Phallu and Hesron and Charmi.

AJC The sons of Simeon: Jamuel and Jamin and Ahod, and Jachin and Sohar, and Saul the son of a Canaanite.

AJC The sons of Levi: Gerson and Caath and Merari.
